About the college

ABOUT COLLEGE SCTCE had the privilege of being one of the top seven colleges in the state aided under the World Bank funded Technical Education Quality Improvement Program (TEQIP)-phase I of the Government of India. The college was ranked as one among the top engineering colleges in India in many national surveys conducted by media such as OUTLOOK, THE WEEK etc. SCTCE is the only engineering college selected for bus body testing and certification Centre by Govt. of Kerala in association with KSRTC as per AIS 52 standards. Many innovative projects by students have been adjudged as best at various levels –one in Limca Books of world records

Vision and Mission of the College

VISION
To become an engineering and Technology institution which is renowned for producing professionally capable and socially responsible engineers
MISSION
To create a learning process for students to acquire engineering fundamentals, in an environment that encourages analysis, team work, entrepreneurship and ethical values, thus preparing them for productive careers.

Vision and Mission of Department

VISION
To be a centre of quality education and research in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering, to mould socially responsible engineering professionals.
MISSION
Provide a systematic teaching-learning process, aiming quality education, in an ambience that encourages research, industry interaction and value based education.
